FOOTBALL


Football is the world's most popular sport and is played by two teams of 11 players. The object of the game is to score by getting the spherical ball into the opposing goal.Its modern origins began in 1863 in England when the Football Association (FA) established the first standardized rules, separating it from rugby. While ancient, less-codified forms like Chinese Cuju existed 2,000+ years ago, the game was refined in Victorian public schools.Then it rapidly became a global, professional sport, with FIFA founded in 1904.
